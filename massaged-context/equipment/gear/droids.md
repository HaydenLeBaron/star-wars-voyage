EDIT
Droids are numerous and varied, with even more models available than those detailed in the tables below. They are a mainstay of the Star Wars universe and can serve as self-directed tools for characters. Of course, virtually all droids are also sentient and with the potential to be another character accompanying the group, despite also being a commodity the player characters can purchase.

The tables below are arbitrarily split for ease of finding what droid model you might want. Due to the limitation of tables, only some aspects of the droid are present and you will need to delve further to see the nuances of each model.
