# Transportation Mod

source: https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Transportation_Mod

Attachments for starships and vehicles can be subdivided by what class of transportation they are suited for. Most are capable of being mounted to either, but due to the starship's operations outside the atmosphere and occasional ability to enter hyperspace, and conversely, the widespread use of repulsorlifts in vehicles, some attachments are uniquely only for each of these. Further still, podracers have highly tuned attachments that only work on them due to the particular construction.

In some cases, there isn't clarity on whether models exist for both vehicles and starships; and in some cases, while not specified that it only functions with starships, it is implied by the attachment's effect (such as affecting hyperdrive). The GM has ultimate say on whether an attachment can be fitted when not explicitly stated. Also, remember that there are further requirements for many of these attachments, especially as to the size of vessel that they can be fitted to.
