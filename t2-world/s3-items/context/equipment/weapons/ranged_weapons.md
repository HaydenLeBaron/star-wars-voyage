# Ranged Weapon

source: https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Ranged_Weapon

Ranged weapons come in all shapes and sizes; rifles, pistols, throwing daggers, grenades, and so many more exotic and elaborate weapons. In the tables below, categorised by arbitrary narrative lines (energy vs projectile vs ordnance, etc.), are all the ranged weapons presented across the main core books and supplements for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game.

The profiles presented in these tables are not the full story! Two otherwise identical weapons may have significant narrative implications found on its linked page or in the information about the weapon located in the appropriate book or Wookieepedia.
