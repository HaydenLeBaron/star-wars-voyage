EDIT
While characters are assumed to have their personal clothing items as a matter of course, and the GM should encourage characters to consider the narrative implications of their mundane outfit, the outfits presented in the tables below are broadly categorised as Armor. They are the key source of additional Defenseand Soak for a character, yet not all do so, fitting more like type of clothing or mundane outfit with explicit mechanical effects.

The categories presented here are drawn from the narrative of these armor sets and not a hard rule presented in the rulebook. Most outfits have more effects than their shared characteristics and attributes, which could be guessed at from their name.
