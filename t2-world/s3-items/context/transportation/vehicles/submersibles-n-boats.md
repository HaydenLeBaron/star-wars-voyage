Water vehicles are often submersibles, as most planets will employ some sort of airspeeder to travel over large bodies of water. However, there are also a small number of speeders that are produced specifically for traversing bodies of water.

Some models listed in the table are actually classified as an airspeeder or landspeeder.

Submersibles
There is a small number of specially produced models of submersible, due to the small number of amphibious species which exist in the galaxy on a large scale.
