# Transportation

VEHICLES
Vehicles are controlled through use of the Piloting (Planetary) skill and range from the ancient wheeled cars to starship-like high atmospheric airspeeders. Each of these type of vehicle has its purpose and history to account for in the narrative.

STARSHIPS
Starships are the quintessential medium of transportation, used with the Piloting (Space) skill. Starships come in all shapes and sizes, from the hyperdrive-lacking ship's boats and snubfighters, to the great battlecruisers deployed across the galaxy to fight grand battles.

RIDING BEASTS
The riding beast is an oddity among forms of transportation, as it is widely present across the Outer Rim to replace more expensive technology where the creatures are native or imported for other purposes. The logistics follow a completely different chain for this option, and they are controlled by the Survival skill.
