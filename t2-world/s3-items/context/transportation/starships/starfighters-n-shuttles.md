
Starfighters & Patrol Boats

https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Starfighters_%26_Patrol_Boats

The smallest starships are almost all some form of fighter, bomber, shuttle or small cruiser, the latter known as a 'patrol boat'. Choices are so wide and varied that they are hard to compare one-to-one. Most individuals appear to make their choices on either what's available at the time or a model they prefer aesthetically. The tables serve to compile a list, but a not particularly useful for comparisons.

Patrol Boats
These are heavily armed and sometimes also armoured vessels that are the size of light freighters, but fulfill the role of light cruisers in the battlefield. They are typically used in hunting smaller craft by small defense forces and security organisations.
