# Crafting
Category page
SIGN IN TO SAVE
EDIT
Given materials and time, anyone can cobble together some pieces of equipment, and those with expertise can produce some exceptional items at a fraction of the cost of manufactured options. These range from on-the-go dug-out fortifications, to crafting weapons and droids, to the mystical alchemy of force sensitives. If you've got the expertise, there's little limit to what you may create.
