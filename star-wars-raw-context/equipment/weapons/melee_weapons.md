Melee weapons are fundamentally governed by three distinct skills in the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. Brawl-enhancing weapons, which improve a character's unarmed attacks. The traditional and high-tech melee weapons of the galaxy, all some variation of stick or board. The legendary lightsabers, requiring special training, and made custom by their users.

The profiles presented in these tables are not the full story! Two otherwise identical weapons may have significant narrative implications found on its linked page or in the information about the weapon located in the appropriate book or Wookieepedia.
