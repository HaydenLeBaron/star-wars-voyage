# Armor Mod

source: https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Armor_Mod

Armour attachments are mostly based around increasing the utility and features of the armour rather than improve its base function, protecting the user. Ranging from sealing the armour from environmental effects, to the attachment of automated weapons and spikes onto battle armour, there's something here for everyone.
