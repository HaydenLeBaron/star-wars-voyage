While some weapons come designed to the pinnacle of performance right off the production line, there's an array of standard weapons which could do with additional features. Whether you're stripping a weapon down to make it lighter and harder to notice, or you're meddling with some safety features to push that last bit of power out of your blaster, there's something for you.
