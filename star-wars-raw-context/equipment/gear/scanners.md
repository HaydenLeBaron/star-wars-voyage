# Scanning & Surveillance

source: https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Scanning_%26_Surveillance

There is an array of gear used for scanning and surveillance. Ranging from the industrial scanners for use in repairs, construction and exploration; to small and compact surveillance equipment used by intelligence agencies and bounty hunters for tracking their mark. There are plenty of uses for any one piece of equipment in this table.
