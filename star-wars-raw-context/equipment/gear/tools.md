# Tools

source: https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Tools

The term 'tools' is a great catch-all for all the gear which is otherwise hard to categorize. It comprises a lot of equipment that is used by all sorts of people, but also includes some extremely specialized equipment and industrial tools. If there's something you need and couldn't categorize it another way, it'll be here.
