Individuals of this galaxy have an array of pharmaceuticals at their disposal. Ranging from dangerous synthetic and harvested toxins, to euphoria causing spice (drugs), there may be some use in these for anyone who comes by it. However, there often comes a great cost when dealing with this gear.

Spice & Drugs
Most illicit narcotics are known as 'Spice', almost always being addictive, they can often come with short term benefits to their use... which is often what traps otherwise enterprising individuals in the vicious cycle.
