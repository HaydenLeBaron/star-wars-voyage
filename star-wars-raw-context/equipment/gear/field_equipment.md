Field equipment is more-so items you would find around a settlement or hastily assembled field base of sorts. Few of these items can be transported by individuals, often necessitating some vehicle or starship to bring to their ultimate location for use.

Support Structures
These pieces of field equipment all provide additional utility to a modular base, often placed apart from the main structure.
