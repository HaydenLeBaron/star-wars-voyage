# Walker

source: https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Walker

Walkers could be considered archaic when compared to the vehicles which use repulsorlift technologies to fly and hover, however, they are widely used due to their lower energy consumption and versatility in terrain use. Often, these are military, surveyor and colony-bound vehicles used in exploration and combat on outer rim worlds.

Usefully, most walkers are self-explanatory in their name, some details are present in these tables, but most are on the pages themselves.

Military Walkers
Walkers are armoured weapons platforms which perform on a variety of terrains. This explains why every large army iterates on a new design for use in military operations.
