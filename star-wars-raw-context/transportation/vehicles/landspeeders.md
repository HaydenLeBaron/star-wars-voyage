# Landspeeder

source: https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Landspeeder

Landspeeders are the most common form of personal transports in the galaxy, enabled by low power repulsorlift technologies. This allows for a wide variety of craft to be built without the need for wheels, tracks or walker legs. The options available to the denizens of the galaxy are many, with many more points of comparison than can be presented on a simple table. A lot of pertinent information is contained on the actual page linked to on the table.

Swoops & Bikes
Many landspeeders are known as swoops, or more colloquially known as bikes due to their resemblance to the ancient wheeled vehicle known as bicycles. These can often come with built-in weapons for self defense, or offense as many are used by criminals and gangs.
