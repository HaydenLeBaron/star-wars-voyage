# Freighters & Liners

https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Freighters_%26_Liners

Freighters and liners are the starships that make the galaxy turn. Light freighters make up the bulk of individual transports for the smuggler and merchant types, though the majority of freight is completed by bulk and heavy transporters. The latter often need their own escorts as most lack armaments. Liners are similar enough to the freighters, simply fitted with increasingly fancy passenger cabins.

The most important factors for freighters tend to be weapons and hyperdrive ratings, but there is a lot of subjective flavour that comes into picking one from the table.
