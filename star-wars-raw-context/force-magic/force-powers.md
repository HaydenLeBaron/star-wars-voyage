# Force Powers


https://star-wars-rpg-ffg.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Force_Power

For those sensitive to the force that surrounds them, there are additional options with which to interact with the world. Whether one learns these through meditation and finding your own power, or one learns from a mentor, these can be powerful tools.

4	Popular Force Powers
World Manipulation
Alter
Mold the natural world around yourself.

Conjure
Summon facsimiles of the real world.

Misdirect
Create illusions in the mind of others.

Protect/Unleash
Absorb or release energy at will.

Suppress
Interfere with others' manipulation of the force.

Control Beings & Objects
Bind
Restrain and crush your opponents.

Influence
Shape the mind of others.

Manipulate
Mold mechanical components at will.

Move
Manipulate items with your mind.

Empowerment
Battle Meditation
Empower your allies in battle.

Ebb/Flow
Flow energy to or from those around you.

Endure
Fight through pain and injury.

Enhance
Empower your bodily self.

Farsight
View the world through the lens of the force.

Foresee
Gain glimpses of the future.

Warde's Foresight
Signature twist on the Foresee power.

Heal/Harm
Bring life, or take it away.

Imbue
Empower an allies body.

Seek
Gain insight into the way of the universe.

Sense
Feel the world about you.
