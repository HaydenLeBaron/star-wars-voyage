# Species

The galaxy is full of species. Ranging from small technologically isolated Ewok, to the extremely versatile Human, to the biggest of the Hutt; all these have their strengths and weaknesses. Evidently, these are but the blank slates - doesn't mean a small Ewok can't train themselves to be as strong as their apparent cousins the Wookiees.
